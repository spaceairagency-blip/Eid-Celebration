The Fifth Pillar

Hajj Guide

Understanding the rites and rituals of the blessed Hajj pilgrimage

Ihram
The sacred state entered before performing Hajj or Umrah. White seamless garments representing equality before Allah.
Tawaf
Circumambulating the Kaaba seven times counterclockwise, starting from the Black Stone (Hajar al-Aswad).
Sa'i
Walking seven times between the hills of Safa and Marwa, commemorating Hajar's search for water.
Arafah
Standing at the plain of Arafah on the 9th of Dhul Hijjah — the pinnacle and heart of Hajj.
Muzdalifah
Spending the night under the open sky, collecting pebbles for the Rami (stoning) at Mina.
Rami
Stoning the three Jamarat pillars at Mina, symbolizing Ibrahim's rejection of Shaytan's temptations.
